Nice to meet you! I'm Brandon.

I'm 21 years old and live in Poole, Dorset. I'm hard working guy with a can-do attitude. I'm always looking to learn more and do more.

I design. I create.

I've always been creative, ever since a child. I have always used creativity as an outlet for my wondering thoughts and ideas. I drifted towards computing since the age of 12 by creating graphics and logos, programming small games and making videos. This led me down more complex routes, creating and designing webpages for real clients, learning the programming language LUA and creating vector based logos and branding.

Other stuff about me.

Other than this, I enjoy self-development, working out, cooking and learning new skills. I love to try and be the best version of myself, so I practice meditation, journal and try to stay fit and healthy. I moved out at the young age of just 17, I rented a 1 bedroom flat and was completly independant as well as working full time. I learned a lot of valuable skills during my time living alone. I learnt to cook, look after myself and build a great work ethic (and how to use a washing machine).
